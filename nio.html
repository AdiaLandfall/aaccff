Whisper quiet and easily missed, Nio blends into the shadows, hides in plain sight, and can cross a pond without a ripple.

Pale, tall, wiry, and some wavering, she is the newest member of The Blue, with a past not yet fully understood. Welcomed gladly, she has grown from a terrified and distrusting shell to a proud and steady fighter, lover, and explorer.

She dresses mostly in black, favouring simple and subdued coats or tunics.

Her most striking feature are those gleaming eyes, peering from the dark, twin turns of silver as bright as the full moon each.

While she is quiet, she can be coaxed into conversation, her voice as soft as feathers upon snow.
