Corpae

As races go, Viera are quite tall, yet Adia is positively towering, overshadowed only by the largest of Roegadyn and Au Ra, and her ears add another fulm at least. With intense and large green eyes, a great head of midnight blue hair, shot through with a bright cream, and perfectly pale skin, the "Didact" of The Blue is very hard to miss in a crowd.

Although her form boasts only some signs of muscle, slight in frame, and lacking curves, her presence in battle is as a force of nature: rapid, powerful, and steady.

When gracing the market square, her every interaction is crowned with a calm and sunny smile, her eyes open windows to the gentle and selfless soul within.

Dressing casual, often to a fault, she prefers prim and elegant attire for combat: simple jackets and pants, solid shoes, and weapon on show. This is all to ensure any can identify her well: the Warrior of Light, fit for duty and eager to serve her Eorzea.

Gravae

Adia is the link between Eorzea and The Blue. While the other Members can be a little fish-out-of-water, Adia has had every opportunity to soak in the culture, the history, the language, and make Eorzea her home.

If you are curious about The Blue, their inner workings and day to day, Adia is the one to ask!

As the Warrior of Light, Adia is also humbled and happy to discuss her Echo and her Blessing of Light (as well as the differences!). She's especially eager to chatter with others who share those gifts!

Lastly, if you are brave enough to pry about her past, Adia tends to be the most open about The Blue's place of origin.
