Corpae

Anzille is at once the heart, the technical genius, the dead-eye shot, and the most frail member of The Blue.

Small even for a Miqo'te, brightly pale skin, a shock of blonde hair, and a affection for intense colours, The Aug stands out in a crowd in her own way. If she is not seen, then she is heard, her voice powerful, bubbly and melodic.

Her eyes are her most dramatic feature: a prominent cerulean blue, paired with a rich blood-red. Her red eye is in fact, a prosthetic, fashioned by her own hands from an enormous ruby gifted to her by an aspiring miner.

Gravae

The Mechan, The Aug, The Eye, Anzille carries a lot of reputation, and she will be endlessly thrilled by any who share them.

An engineer, she builds and tinkers creates and shapes, and will talk for hours about her projects without pause.

Her eye is just one of those projects, and she wears it proudly. As long as one is polite, she is commonly excited to elaborate on its features and construction.

Lastly, her title of Aug is well earned and surprisingly complex a subject. Surely gentle inquiry will lead to enlightening, if confounding conversation.
