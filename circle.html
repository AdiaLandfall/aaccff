While the gathering of individuals known as The Blue are made up of a diverse people, there are stripes that can be painted broad across them all. For such detail, and to provide an introduction:

Eidenae (History)

The Blue are not native to the star of Etheirys. While their origins remain mysterious for the very most part, a simple recount is possible. Five years after the Calamity of the Seventh Umbral Era, a strange Viera woman arrived on Lominsan shores: one Adia. Months later, and separated by months each, others arrived, and the Circle grew slowly to its current size. The Blue are undoubtedly borne collectively of another place, one with names, places, and a complex history.

Gravae (Hooks)

The Blue will, nominally, speak with anyone. Each member takes pride or interest in their own topics, yet generally they will accept the greeting of a stranger without issue. While some are new and adjusting, most are well versed and storied, with their own views on events since their arrival. They will take great interest in anyone who is, like them, not native to Etheirys. Those brave of spirit and open of mind will find their tales of foreign magicks, strange technologies, and odd places to be quite exciting. If you have an adventure to share, a narrative to retell, or a curiosity to dangle, The Blue will answer the call.

Crescerae (OOC)

The Blue are a reflection of a sincere, literal "circle" or "system" of several conscious individuals, sharing a single brain. This phenomena is often referred to as "a plural person", or an expression of "DID". The themes, processes, and realities of such a group tend to be expressed through both language and action while enjoying the glorious realm of Eorzea, and beyond.
