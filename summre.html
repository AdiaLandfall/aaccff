Among the towering cliffs of other members of The Blue, Summre does not number. Yet he does stand over the shorter kin. At a gentle five fulms, the Viera is curious on many fronts and proud of them all.

Not quite a man, nor a woman, they prefer to straddle the line, leaning into gender on occasion. Due to this, one may refer to Summre in any fashion, without issue.

Soft flowing red curls, deep dark eyes, and bold freckles make for a somewhat dreamy appearance, until the smirk arrives, providing a more snarky and sarcastic affair.

Yet for all of Summre's pranks, nicknames, and teasing, he is steadfast in his dedication to The Blue. Sporting everything from sleek suits, to ruffled dresses, polished parade armor and revealing swim-wear, Summre has and will wear anything, for any passing reason, leaning toward blinding reds, soft pinks, white highlights, and well matched hues.
